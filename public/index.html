About Me

I was born in Hong Kong, spent my early years in southern China and the Philippines, spent my school years in the UAE and Jordan and another year in Germany.

So where am I at the moment? I am currently a software developer, in the country of Jordan. Looking back at it, it has been a tough Journey. And is still tougher than ever for each subsequent generation graduating from a lackluster educational body.

My experiences as an energy engineer varied a ton, from manual on-site work, to data analysis of lots of data, to simulative desktop work and even a white collar internship, to throw into the mix.

When I have looked back on these experiences, I was thinking of where I am heading. I was inspired to make the switch, as soon as corona started when I noticed that all of them involved some kind of problem solving. Whether it involved scripting or launching a content, I had a deep desire to learn these technologies to get it done. And now I am in my own way of trying to solve the problem, that is educational hurdles in Jordan, which has inspired self learning and eventual enrolment into the Software Development Path.
